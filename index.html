Introduction

Counter-Strike is a highly popular first-person shooter (FPS) game that has captured the attention of players worldwide since its initial release in 1999. Known for its tactical gameplay, Counter-Strike pits two teams—Terrorists and Counter-Terrorists—against each other in high-stakes matches. The game demands strategy, teamwork, and precision, with objectives like planting or defusing bombs, rescuing hostages, or eliminating the enemy team.

I find it appealing due to it's competitive nature. The adrenaline of fast-paced rounds, and thriving community with active esports tournaments. It takes me away from problemsolving for a short while, thus giving my brain rest. I've made alot of friends (are they really?) whilst playing this game.
